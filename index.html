About Me

I am a research engineer at Huawei (from Nov. 2016). I received my PhD in Information Engineering at The Chinsese University of Hong Kong in 2016, supervised by Prof. Soung Chang LIEW. Previously, I received my B.Sc. and M.E. degrees in Computer Science from Nanjing University.

My research interests are broadly in experimental wireless networks, computer networks and distributed systems. I am currently focusing on

  • Network Verification (Data Center Networks, …)

  • IoT Systems (LPWAN, WiFi, WSN, Bluetooth, …)
