About Me

I am an Assistant Professor with School of Informatics, Xiamen University, China, starting from Jan. 2021. Before that, I was a research engineer at Huawei 2012 Labs (from Nov. 2016 to Dec. 2020). I received my PhD degree in Information Engineering at The Chinsese University of Hong Kong in 2016, supervised by Prof. Soung Chang Liew. Previously, I received my BSc and ME degrees in Computer Science both from Nanjing University, supervised by Prof. Guihai Chen.

My research interests span wireless/computer networks, network/data security, and edge AI. I am particularly motivated by challenging problems in real-world networked systems, with a focus on developing practical solutions.

I work closely with the team led by Prof. Liqun Fu and the team led by Prof. Qiao Xiang.
